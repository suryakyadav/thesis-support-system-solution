The Thesis Journey: A Shared Challenge

As educators, administrators, and supervisors, we all share a common goal: to see our students succeed. Yet, the thesis process is often a point of friction — at every level.

Whether it's an undergraduate dissertation, a Master's thesis, or a doctoral project, the same structural breakdown occurs: students lose momentum, supervisors lose time, and institutions lose students.

34%

EUA Council for Doctoral Education, 2019

of doctoral candidates who enrolled in 2009 had not completed within six years — and fewer than half of those who did finish met the target timeframe of 3–4 years.

For Students

Talented students feel lost and unmotivated, struggling to navigate a complex and often isolating process.

For Supervisors

Supervisors are overwhelmed with administrative tasks, leaving less time for high-impact academic mentorship.

For Universities

Each non-completing thesis student — undergraduate, Master's, or doctoral — represents an estimated €10,000–€15,000 in unrecovered tuition, sunk institutional overhead, and recruitment costs. The reputational cost to rankings is additional.

Questions institutions ask before joining

Your institution owns all data, always. Aveksana acts as a data processor under your institution's data controller authority. Your data is never used to train AI models or shared with third parties. A Data Processing Agreement (DPA) is provided as standard before any pilot begins.
The 12-week institutional pilot is provided at no cost. Our model is partnership-first: we prove the value using your real cohort data before any commercial conversation takes place. Post-pilot pricing is agreed transparently, based on enrolled student count.
For SSO and basic LMS read-access, typically 2–3 working days with your IT team. Full integration — assignment sync, grade passback, SIS connection — takes 2–4 weeks depending on your LMS configuration. We provide dedicated technical support throughout and do not require your IT team to carry the process alone.
Your supervisors do, always. Aveksana surfaces structural completeness, citation gaps, and argument flow — it flags these for the supervisor's consideration. The platform makes no automated grade, pass, or fail decision. Academic judgement on quality, merit, and originality remains entirely with your faculty.
